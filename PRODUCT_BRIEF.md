# Product Brief

## Working Name

Career Transition OS

## Problem

Switching jobs is messy. A person has to manage job listings, tailored resumes, cover letters, recruiter messages, referrals, interviews, follow-ups, salary notes, and decisions across many companies. Most people end up using scattered spreadsheets, docs, email, calendar, and memory.

## Target User

A professional actively moving from Job A to Job B who wants one private place to manage the whole transition.

## Core Promise

Help the user stay organized, prepared, and responsive through every stage of a job switch.

## Main Jobs To Be Done

- Track every opportunity from saved role to final decision.
- Remember what resume and cover letter were sent to each company.
- Prepare for recruiter calls and interviews.
- Know which follow-ups are due.
- Compare opportunities and offers.
- Keep a searchable history of the whole transition.

## MVP Features

- Mobile dashboard
- Opportunity pipeline
- Opportunity detail page
- Resume and cover-letter tracker
- Interview planner
- Follow-up reminders
- Basic analytics

## Differentiation

This is not just an applicant tracker. It is a full transition workspace that connects applications, documents, people, conversations, preparation, and decision-making.

## Design Principles

- Mobile-first and daily-use friendly
- Calm, focused, and practical
- Clear next actions over visual noise
- Fast capture first, detailed organization second
- Every opportunity should have a complete story
